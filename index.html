ABOUT ME

Tracy Tai


Welcome to my website. My name is Tracy Tai. I am a college student currently studying Psychology and Informatics in University of Washington, Seattle. Seattle is a beautiful place with rainy days, which are my favorite weather. I love to sit beside my window and listen to how rain drops hit the ground. It makes me calm and peaceful. I love to draw different things during my free time, especially under the pandemic. I can express my emotions through creating different characters and objects. I have included many of my design and coding projects and drawings in this website. Hope you enjoy!

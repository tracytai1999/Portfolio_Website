GoodNight Mobile App Prototype

Click to see the prototype.


This prototype was created upon considering sleep deprivations among college students. College students get irregular hours of sleep due to an overload of activities including classes, work, clubs, and maintaining a social life. Most college students have the option of using sleep tracking apps or devices, but these software are typically designed for a wide range of people. Lack of sleep has also been shown to result in lower productivity, starting a vicious cycle of choosing study over sleep, resulting in lower quality of studying and thus more time needed to study. This in turn causes students to forgo even more sleep. This is a clear problem for college students, especially those who have jobs and extracurricular activities. We believe that a solution is necessary to specifically target college students and ensure they get an acceptable amount of quality sleep per night depending on upcoming events (midterms, finals, or a 10-hour shift at work). This prototype aimed to help college students maintain a healthy sleep schedule.
